 Double Wishbone Suspension Design
NTHURacing TH07, National Tsing Hua University

project page / video

Designed and manufactured a double-wishbone suspension with an integrated anti-roll bar for TH07, a 4WD electric racecar. Applied Design for Manufacturing in SolidWorks and used FEA to optimise the weight-to-stiffness ratio.
